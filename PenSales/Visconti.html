Opera Club

This is the older style Opera known as the Opera Club (circa early 2000s). These have semi-flexible 14 kt nibs rather than the 23kt Palladium nibs in more recent Operas. This one comes with a typically smooth medium nib. It is a very elegant pen; understated and professional looking. The filling system is cartridge converter. It has never been used, and comes with all the boxes, including the external sleeve. The ball point takes the usual Parker-style refill; it is twist mechanism. The pens have the My Pen System, so your dealer can replace the Visconti jewels with your initials. I have seen only a few of these and the FPs go in the $425-525 range.

FP: $400; BP: $175

Copernicus

Green is No. 270 and Blue is No. 12.

Blue: $425; Green: $375
