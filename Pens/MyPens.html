My Pen Collection

I collect fountain pens (well, actually any kind of stationery). I keep telling myself that I focus on three brands: Pelikan, Visconti, and Sheaffer and one line: Montblanc Writer Series,but this "focus" should not be taken too seriously. I seem to have a real difficulty in taking a pass on a pen that I really like. As a result, I have a varying collection. The following pages show my selection. By definition, these pages are continually under construction. However, even the pens that I have will be added here as I can scan them and insert them into my database. Enjoy perusing them and if you notice any errors regarding dates, etc, please let me know by sending me email at mtozsu@gmail.com.
