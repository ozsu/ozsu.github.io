Sheaffer Collection

Sheaffer is one of the more difficult brands to collect. There are so many models that it is simply very difficult to keep track of them all. I started collecting them simply because I love the inlaid nibs (as you can tell from the number of Targa's, Crest's and Legacy's I have) and some of the vintage brands are both beautiful and ingenious in their engineering (such as the Snorkel). Despite the relatively large number of pens I have, my Sheaffer collection is not well organized. The following are in alphabetic order.
